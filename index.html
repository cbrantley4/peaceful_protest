Protesting is our right as Americans

According to the United States Constitution:

Article the third... Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof; or abridging the freedom of speech, or of the press; or the right of the people peaceably to assemble, and to petition the Government for a redress of grievances.

Found from https://www.archives.gov/founding-docs/bill-of-rights-transcript

In the times of the Pandemic:

For your health, and the health of the people around you, people should not be gathering in groups. This site was created so that people can exercise their right to protest without physically being in groups.
